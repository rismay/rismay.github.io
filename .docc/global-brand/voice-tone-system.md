  Shared voice and tone standards across all brands.
  ## Voice
  - Clear, direct, and technically precise.
  - Favor active voice and short sentences.
  ## Tone
  - Calm, confident, and grounded.
  - Avoid hype and exaggerated claims.
  ## Word bank
  - build, ship, clarify, structure, audit, validate
  ## Do
  - "Build what you can audit."
  - "Make the system legible."
  ## Don't
  - "The most revolutionary thing ever."
  - "Magic and mystery solve everything."
}
