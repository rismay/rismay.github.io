  Shared accessibility standards across all brands.
  ## Contrast
  - Minimum 4.5:1 for body text.
  - Minimum 3:1 for large text.
  ## Motion
  - Respect reduced motion settings.
  - Avoid forced parallax or autoplay.
  ## Semantics
  - Use correct heading order.
  - Provide descriptive link labels.
  - Ensure focus states are visible.
  ## Targets
  - Minimum hit target: 44x44.
  - Keyboard navigation must reach all interactive elements.
}
