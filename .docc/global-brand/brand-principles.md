  Shared rules that apply to every brand unless a brand page explicitly overrides
  this document.
  ## Design foundations
  - **Type-first:** typography sets hierarchy; decoration never replaces structure.
  - **Geometric structure:** layouts align to grids and crisp edges.
  - **Color discipline:** prefer restrained palettes with a clear primary signal.
  - **Contrast by default:** meet or exceed 4.5:1 for body text.
  - **Motion with intent:** animation clarifies hierarchy and state, never novelty.
  ## Documentation standards
  - Keep DocC files kebab-case and lowercase.
  - Use a landing page plus topic subpages for each brand.
  - Store shared assets in bundle `Resources/`.
  ## Governance
  - Treat this page as the global baseline.
  - Brand pages may only diverge with explicit rationale.
}
