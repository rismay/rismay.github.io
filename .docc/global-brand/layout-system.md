  Shared layout rules across all brands.
  ## Spacing scale
  - 4, 8, 12, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96
  ## Grid
  - Desktop: 12 columns, 24px gutters
  - Tablet: 6 columns, 20px gutters
  - Mobile: 4 columns, 16px gutters
  ## Structure
  - Favor clear sectional blocks.
  - Align text baselines where possible.
  - Avoid visual noise in favor of hierarchy and whitespace.
}
