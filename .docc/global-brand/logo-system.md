  Shared logo rules across all brands.
  ## Required assets
  - Primary wordmark (SVG)
  - Monogram or mark (SVG)
  - Favicon or app icon (SVG/PNG)
  ## Clear space
  - Minimum padding: 1x the height of the primary mark.
  ## Usage sizes
  - Minimum wordmark width: 120px
  - Minimum monogram width: 24px
  ## Misuse
  - No gradients unless the brand defines them.
  - No skew, outlines, or shadows.
  - Do not stretch or distort logos.
}
