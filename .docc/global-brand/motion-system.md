  Shared motion standards across all brands.
  ## Timing
  - Micro transitions: 120-200ms
  - Section transitions: 240-360ms
  ## Curves
  - **Standard:** `cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.8, 0.2, 1)`
  - **Exit:** `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 1, 1)`
  ## Usage
  - Motion should clarify structure or state.
  - Keep motion minimal for focus-heavy content.
  - Respect reduced motion preferences.
}
