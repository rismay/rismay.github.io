## Intent

The logo emphasizes modularity and composability across the CommonSwift
libraries.

## Primary mark

- Use the primary logo for marketing pages, docs landing pages, and app
  about screens.
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the logo mark.

## Secondary marks

- Use monochrome marks for small UI surfaces or low-contrast contexts.
- Avoid stacking marks unless the layout requires vertical alignment.

## Do

- Keep the logo in high-contrast contexts.
- Use SVG assets whenever possible.

## Avoid

- Distorting the logo proportions.
- Placing the logo over busy backgrounds.
