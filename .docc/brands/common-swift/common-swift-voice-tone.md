## Intent

CommonSwift speaks with calm confidence and technical clarity.

## Voice

- Precise and practical.
- Minimal hype, maximum clarity.
- Emphasize dependability and composability.

## Tone by context

- Docs and guides: clear, instructional, unhurried.
- Release notes: concise, factual, action-oriented.
- Marketing: confident, still grounded in specifics.

## Do

- Use active voice and short sentences.
- Explain why a tool exists before how it works.

## Avoid

- Overly casual language or exaggerated claims.
- Long paragraphs that hide key actions.
