Define the typography standards for the Studio Laussat brand.

@Image(source: "brands-laussat-studio-laussat-studio-typography-hero", alt: "studio laussat: typography hero")

## Principles

- Serif-forward voice for editorial polish and craft.
- High contrast and generous spacing for quiet confidence.
- Minimal weights; emphasize optical size and rhythm.

## Specifications

- **Primary display serif:** Canela (preferred), or Cormorant Garamond.
- **Primary text serif:** Iowan Old Style (system), or Source Serif 4.
- **Fallback stack:** "Iowan Old Style", "Times New Roman", "Cormorant Garamond", serif.
- **Weights:** Regular and Semibold only; avoid heavy blacks.
- **Tracking:** +2 to +6 for all-caps accents; default tracking for body.
