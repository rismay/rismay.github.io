Define the iconography standards for the Studio Laussat brand.

@Image(source: "brands-laussat-studio-laussat-studio-iconography-hero", alt: "studio laussat: iconography hero")

## Principles

- Monoline geometry with soft joins.
- No fills; strokes only.
- Keep icons editorial, not playful.

## Specifications

- **Stroke:** 2.5px at 24x24.
- **Corner radius:** 2px minimum.
