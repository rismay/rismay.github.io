Define the color standards for the Studio Laussat brand.

@Image(source: "brands-laussat-studio-laussat-studio-color-hero", alt: "studio laussat: color hero")

## Principles

- Grayscale only; rely on contrast, texture, and typography.
- Avoid saturated accents unless required for accessibility.
- Use warm neutrals to keep the palette human, not clinical.

## Specifications

- **Ink:** #1B1B1B
- **Midnight:** #2C2C2C
- **Slate:** #4A4A4A
- **Mist:** #B6B6B6
- **Fog:** #DADADA
- **Paper:** #F2F2F2
- **White:** #FFFFFF
