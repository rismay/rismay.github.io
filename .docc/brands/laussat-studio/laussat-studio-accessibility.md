Define the accessibility standards for the Studio Laussat brand.

@Image(source: "brands-laussat-studio-laussat-studio-accessibility-hero", alt: "studio laussat: accessibility hero")

## Principles

- Grayscale must pass contrast in every state.
- Serif text stays legible at small sizes and low vision settings.
- Motion is optional; clarity is mandatory.

## Specifications

- **Body contrast:** 4.5:1 minimum.
- **Large text contrast:** 3:1 minimum.
- **Focus states:** 2px outline, #1B1B1B on light surfaces.
