## Purpose

CodeSwiftly is a focused, local-first learning and coding companion. The brand
should feel friendly, calm, and precise with a baby-blue trust signal.

## Audience

Developers and learners who want an IDE-like reading and practice experience
without noise or hype.

## Principles

- Calm and inviting
- Precise and technical
- Local-first, privacy-respecting
- Companionable without being casual
