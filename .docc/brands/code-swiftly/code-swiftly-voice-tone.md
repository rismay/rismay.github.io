## Intent

CodeSwiftly speaks like a calm, precise learning companion.

## Voice

- Clear, direct, and technically accurate.
- Supportive without being casual.
- Focus on actionable guidance.

## Tone by context

- Reader: calm and explanatory.
- Exercises: encouraging, precise, and clear about errors.
- Release notes: concise and factual.

## Do

- Use active voice and short sentences.
- Explain why a step matters.

## Avoid

- Overly playful or sarcastic language.
- Long paragraphs that bury the next action.
