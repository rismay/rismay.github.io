  Minimal wordmark and geometric monogram.
  ## Wordmark
  - Lowercase "rismay" set in Space Grotesk 700.
  - Prefer black or a single primary color.
  ## Monogram
  - Single "r" built from a square grid.
  - Use for avatars and compact badges.
  ## Clear space
  - Minimum padding: 1x the height of the lowercase "r".
  ## Misuse
  - No outlines, gradients, or drop shadows.
  - Do not skew or add rounded corners.
}
