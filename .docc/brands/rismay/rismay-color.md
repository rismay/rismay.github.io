  Primary colors + hard neutrals. Use color blocks and strong contrast.
  ## Palette
  - **Red:** `#E10600`
  - **Blue:** `#0047FF`
  - **Yellow:** `#FFD400`
  - **Black:** `#0A0A0A`
  - **White:** `#F9F9F9`
  ## Rules
  - Use one primary color per layout section.
  - Pair each primary with black or white for contrast.
  - Avoid gradients and muted variants unless needed for accessibility.
  - Never combine red and blue in body copy; reserve for headlines or accents.
  ## Contrast targets
  - Body text: minimum 4.5:1
  - Headlines: minimum 3:1 (prefer 4.5:1)
}
