  Direct, minimal, and technically precise.
  ## Voice
  - Confident and concise.
  - Use short sentences and active voice.
  - Prefer clear nouns over metaphors.
  ## Tone
  - Calm, analytical, forward-looking.
  - Avoid hype and excessive adjectives.
  ## Do
  - "Build systems that outlive projects."
  - "Shipping requires repeatable structure."
  ## Don't
  - "We are the most revolutionary platform ever."
  - "Magic happens here."
}
